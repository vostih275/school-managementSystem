About AIC Lokichoggio Girls Primary & Junior

Established in the mid-1990s as part of the Africa Inland Church’s (AIC) mission, AIC Lokichoggio Girls has been a pillar of hope and academic excellence in Turkana. Operating as a public, church-sponsored day school, we were founded with a specific purpose: to provide safe, accessible education for girls in a region where female enrollment was historically low. Today, we provide an engaging space where approximately 160 girls thrive.
